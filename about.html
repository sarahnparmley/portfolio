About Me

Hi, I'm Sarah!

I got my start in design during high school and quickly realized it was more than just a hobby. As I worked toward my degree in Integrated Strategic Communication and Psychology at the University of Kentucky, I kept designing, taking on freelance projects that let me explore everything from branding and social content to UI and web design.

Design has become the way I think through ideas and connect with people. Whether it's building out a brand, creating marketing visuals, or designing a clean user interface, I’m drawn to work that’s both thoughtful and visually strong.

I’m always excited to take on new design projects, let’s chat.
